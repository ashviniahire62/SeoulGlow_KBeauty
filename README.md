# ✨ Premium DTC K-Beauty: Meta Ads Campaign Proposal

Welcome to the comprehensive campaign repository for **SeoulGlow Skincare**. This repository houses the strategic planning, targeting architecture, and performance tracking framework designed to launch and scale a direct-to-consumer (DTC) acquisition channel in the competitive US skincare market.

---

## 📌 1. Executive Summary & Business Overview

SeoulGlow Skincare is entering the US skincare market by explicitly targeting consumers looking for the highly coveted "Korean glass skin" effect. This proposal outlines a data-driven Meta Ads strategy designed to build brand awareness, drive engagement, and generate scalable DTC sales for our flagship product.

* 🎯 **Primary Objective:** Drive online sales conversions for the Hero product via the e-commerce store.
* 👥 **Secondary Objective:** Build brand equity, increase social media engagement, and gather Meta Pixel data for future retargeting funnels.

## 🎯 3. Target Audience Architecture

To maximize ad spend efficiency and keep customer acquisition costs low, the targeting matrix focuses heavily on users exhibiting high intent and a strong affinity for Korean beauty trends and established beauty retailers.

* 👩 **Demographics:**
  * **Age:** 18–35
  * **Gender:** Female
  * **Location:** United States
* 🔍 **Detailed Targeting (Interest Clusters):**
  * `K-beauty (Korean Beauty)`
  * `Sephora`
  * `Ulta Beauty`
  * `Cosmetics & Skincare interests`

## 🎬 4. Creative Asset Specifications

The creative strategy deploys two distinct, high-converting formats engineered to disrupt the user's social feed and seamlessly guide them down the purchase funnel.

### 🎥 Creative Format 1: UGC Video
* **Format Type:** Short-form vertical video (Optimized for Reels and Instagram Stories).
* **Hook Line:** *"If you are still trying to get Korean glass skin without rice water... stop."*
* **Concept Visuals:** A relatable creator captures a casual video highlighting dull skin, applies the SeoulGlow Serum, and transitions smoothly to reveal a highly hydrated, glowing, and radiant finish.
* **Call to Action (CTA):** `Shop Now`

### 🗂️ Creative Format 2: Carousel Ad
* **Format Type:** Multi-image swipable cards.
* **Card 1 Visual:** High-quality studio shot of the product packaging. *(Text overlay: Glass Skin Rice Water Serum — $28.00)*
* **Card 2 Visual:** Close-up macro shot of product texture and ingredients list, highlighting **Fermented Rice Water, 5% Niacinamide, and Hyaluronic Acid**.
* **Card 3 Visual:** Side-by-side split showing real consumer before-and-after results illustrating faded hyperpigmentation in 7 days.
* **Call to Action (CTA):** `Learn More`
